## 👋 About Me

I hold a Master’s degree in Machine Learning from the Brno University of Technology, graduating with distinction at both bachelor’s and master’s levels. I currently work as a mid-level AI/ML Software Engineer at NXP Semiconductors with 2.5+ years of experience. I won the 2025 iGEM grand prize along with three special prizes and two nominations.

My work focuses on deploying machine learning models on resource‑constrained and embedded platforms, bridging the gap between cutting‑edge AI research and real‑world silicon. I have hands‑on experience with PyTorch, ExecuTorch, and the end‑to‑end optimization of ML pipelines for MCU‑class devices, including model compression, quantization, and runtime performance tuning.
